Popular Group Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Popular group, a pioneer in the distribution of two-wheeler spares parts had a humble beginning in the year 1978 with an employee strength of 10. Today, Popular Group, is the biggest two wheelers spare part distributor in the country, having an employee strength of 400+.

Popular group is distributing esteemed OEM parts for TVS, HONDA, ROYAL ENFIELD, YAMAHA, PIAGGIO. Added to this Popular Group is the leading distributor of reputed brands like ROLON, SUPRAJIT, VARROC, ENDURANCE, NINKI, FIEM, SANDHAR, BANDO, HTA, CEAT, SKF, SHELL, ETC.

Today popular group has expanded and operates from Chennai (1,10,000 sq.ft Warehouse), Madurai (25,000 sq.ft Warehouse) and Salem (4,000sq.ft Warehouse) .Popular Group has a Retailer Network of more than 3,500 Retailers in the State of Tamil Nadu.We at Popular Group ensure that all orders received from our retailers are delivered within the next working day,thus ensuring “Availability,Affordability,Accessibility” for our retailers.

Popular Group is also Importing and Exporting Two Wheeler spare parts
