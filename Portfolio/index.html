UI/UX STEPS

How i made user-friendly design

Research and planning

This step involves conducting research to understand the needs and goals of the target users, as well as the business objectives of the product or service being designed. This might include activities such as user interviews, focus groups, and stakeholder meetings.

Design and prototyping

In this step, designers create wireframes, prototypes, and other design artifacts to visualize and test ideas for the user interface and user experience. This might include creating mockups of screens, defining navigation flows, and creating interactive prototypes.

Testing and iteration

In this step, designers test the design concepts with real users to gather feedback and identify areas for improvement. This might include conducting usability testing, A/B testing, and other types of user research. Based on the feedback received, designers iterate on the design to improve the user experience.

Implementation

In the final step, designers work with developers to turn the approved design into a functional product or service. This might involve creating design specifications, providing design assets (such as icons and graphics), and collaborating with developers to ensure that the final product meets the design vision.
